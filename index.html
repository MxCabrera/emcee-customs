About Us

Emcee Customs was created in 2019 as a safety net to help support myself during the harsh times of the Pandemic. It allowed me to utilize and blend my creativity for art, with my passion for sneakers and the culture. The support I recieved during that time was a blessing and made me realize how much of an impact an exchange so small made both parties feel. I have limited my availability here to part time as I have ventured off into another avenue of life. But for the time being, cheer's to a piece of my time creating the things I loved. Thank you for visiting.

How to Order:

  2. View the selection of services we provide, and click "add to cart" when you want it.

  4. When your satisfied with your choices, click the "order now" button

  5. Fill out the form with the shoe you are providing.
